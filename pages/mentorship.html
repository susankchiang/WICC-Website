Who Can be a Mentor?

Motivated undergraduates, graduate students, and grads majoring in or working CS or related technical fields.

What is required of mentors?

As a mentor you are expected to hold conversations with your mentee monthly, help your mentee establish meaningful connections, give her advice on course selection, or otherwise. The goal of the mentorship program is to motivate young women with interest in computing.
