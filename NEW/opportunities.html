Summer QAmp

SummerQAmp is a nationwide initiative to train a new workforce in tech skills and create tech jobs for America's youth. Learn more at www.summerqamp.org

HackNY

For anyone with coding experience

The hackNY Fellows program, now entering its fifth year, is an intense program designed to introduce students to NYC's startup ecosystem by pairing the best technical minds with great New York startups. Learn more and apply at apply.hackny.org

TechDraft CommonApp

For anyone with code/UX experience

Students can apply to 10 companies with a single application. This program makes it incredibly easy to get to know NYC’s top startups and apply to them all in one place! Apply at techdraft.hirebrite.com/app

Facebook U

For rising college sophomores

FBU is a paid 8-week summer experience to learn about mobile development and get hands-on experience developing features that will help more than a billion people around the world connect to the people and things they care about. During the 8 weeks you’ll get a crash course in mobile engineering and development followed by six weeks of hands-on project experience in a small team setting. Learn more and apply at www.facebook.com/careers/university/fbu

Google Engineering Practicum

For rising college sophomores and juniors

This summer internship program includes a 12-week development project that you will work on in a team of Googlers and other Engineering Practicum interns, skills-based training, professional development, mentorship from Google engineers, and a unique glimpse into applying your computer science studies in a professional environment. Apply at www.google.com/about/jobs/search/?src=Online/TOPs/NA%20Tech%20University#!t=jo&jid=3823002&

Google Code-in

For rising college freshmen

The Google Code-in is a contest to introduce pre-university students (ages 13-17) to the many kinds of contributions that make open source software development possible. The Google Code-in 2013 contest runs from November 18, 2013 to January 6, 2014. Apply at www.google-melange.com/gci/homepage/google/gci2013

Explore Microsoft

For rising college sophomores and juniors

Explore Microsoft is a 12-week summer internship program that is specifically designed for college underclassmen (freshmen and sophomores) and offers the opportunity to experience working at Microsoft. This program is designed to expose students to the field of software development and encourage students to pursue degrees in computer science, computer engineering, or related technical disciplines through hands-on training and group project experience. Learn more at careers.microsoft.com/careers/en/us/internships-explore-microsoft.aspx
